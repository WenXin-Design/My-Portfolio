Cloud Model for Revit

Access cloud model anywhere anytime

Project Introduction

Since architectural modeling cannot be completed by one person or one team, it usually requires multiple disciplines, multiple teams, and collaborative efforts across multiple locations to ultimately complete the project with high quality and ensure the final construction quality. Revit, as the leader in architectural modeling software, has developed a new real-time collaboration model in the cloud, which not only helps architects, structural engineers, MEP engineers to collaborate in real-time on the same model and get real-time feedback, but also helps architectural firms to share and collaborate with clients and construction units in real-time sharing and collaboration, the efficiency of cooperation has been significantly improved.

Requirement analysis

Business goals analysis

Understand the business goals the company is trying to achieve

In terms of the big picture, the vision is to provide a complete and unified cloud-based model browsing, access, and management experience so that cloud-based data can be easily accessed and shared with all project collaborators.

Existing user behavior analysis

Within large companies, users will use Collaboration for Revit or Revit server already provided by Autodesk to collaborate for real-time model sharing and collaboration. But for external collaborators, especially external consultants in small companies, C4R costs an extra $100 per month, which is more than worth the cost for their company size. So for now, the only way to collaborate is to share the models via dropbox, onedrive, email, etc.

Summarize the problems that will be encountered in the whole process based on personas

Throughout the process, not only Ian, but also Ann, with whom he collaborated, was an important participant in the process, so I have included Ann in this chart, and the collaborative process throughout the project shows that the problems they encountered were slightly different, but overlapped mostly.

Ann

  • How to secure the central model? Both at the beginning and after the end of the project.
  • How to quickly share models to external consultants?
  • How do I get instant updates? And make sure everyone is working on the same up-to-date models?

Ian

  • How quickly can you receive a central model from a large company?
  • How can you quickly share your model back to a large company?
  • How do I get instant updates? And make sure everyone is working on the same up-to-date models?
  • How do you manage the models of different companies?
  • How do you manage the different versions of the model?

Storyboard

System map

Ideally, how should the target users work together with the stakeholders?

Based on the current workflow of users drawn before, I reorganized the system diagram of the working relationship between target users and other stakeholders, filled in the problematic parts during the process, and thought comprehensively about how to solve user problems systematically.

From the picture, it can be found that the best solution should be:

  • All the documents of all the people are gathered in one place in the cloud, large companies through C4R and small companies through our new feature to achieve the same purpose.
  • Files in the cloud can reach instant updates, ensuring that all are working on the same up-to-date model.
  • Provides version control and project management features to help target users manage.

Interactive and visual design

Rapid iteration of paper prototypes

Use low cost to achieve rapid iteration

Since there is a lot of uncertainty in the pre-program, I need to use the lowest cost, fastest iterative method, I use in the direct drawing on paper, and then communicate with the PO, development, to ensure the feasibility and rationality of the program.

Interactive design

After the paper prototype is basically agreed upon, we start to output more detailed interaction drafts, including a variety of different solutions, near-term solutions and long-term plans, and need to envision how to implement them step by step if we can't achieve a ideal solution at once.

Visual design

Output visuals according to visual specifications and work closely with development to resolve potential interaction and visual issues.

Test and data

Ensure the usability, user satisfaction and market value of the Feature before and after the official release

In order to ensure that my design meets user expectations and follows user habits, it is essential to carry out user testing, which I have done in three aspects:
