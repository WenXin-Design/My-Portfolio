11.11 Interactive Game Design

Boost the big promotion with games to drive sales with fun

Project Introduction

As Double Eleven became a national shopping carnival, the red envelope game was constantly updated and upgraded. In order to enhance the fun of the Double Eleven event and enhance the retention and pulling ability of Jingdong APP, I took advantage of the social attributes of red envelopes, incorporated the fun elements of the game, combined the dual play of social and breakthrough games, and designed the social breakthrough game red envelope game to add to the increase of Double Eleven sales and eventually achieve the purpose of improving GMV.

Company and Time

JD.com | Nov 2016

Role

Interaction designers, working with visual designers to modify visual design

Result

The cumulative number of game participants reached nearly 8 million, which played a huge role in boosting the final GMV

Requirement Analysis

Requirement background

In addition to launching such activities on Double Eleven, we hope to pull purchases with offers and give users a sense of participation in a fun way, but also hope that more users can participate in the Jingdong Double Eleven to achieve the dual purpose of retention and attract new ones.

Requirements analysis and design positioning

User requirements + business requirements = design orientation

The interaction designer plays a bridging role here, making design decisions that best meet the needs of business, technology and users with a full understanding of both user and business requirements.

Play positioning

Social red envelope + game breakthrough red envelope

The red envelope game has been upgraded with the development of e-commerce, and numerous red envelope games have appeared in recent years, including password red envelope, red envelope rain, AR red envelope, game red envelope, etc. This time, based on the analysis of user needs and business needs, we decided to use the double play of social red envelope + breakthrough game red envelope.

Design difficulties

How to make the game more interesting? How to encourage users to share?

  • On the one hand, it is necessary to give users a certain amount of encouragement at the beginning, so that they have the idea to continue playing. And there should be a suitable evaluation for the difficulty setting of the level, and there should be a perfect combination from the visual and interactive experience to give the user a favorable game experience.
  • On the other hand, it combines attractive benefit points to encourage users to share and get new users involved in the game.

Interactive Design

Function analysis

Based on the user requirements and business needs, combined with the social + game breakthrough red envelope gameplay, I have sorted out the required features.

Process grooming

Interactive design

After the process is defined, the interaction design goes into detail, including not only the process, but also the presentation and organization of information in each page, and the handling of exceptions.

Visual design

Collaborate with visual designers

Work with the visual designer based on design drafts, building the draft visuals together and making sure the visuals match the interaction details.

Visual design

Work closely with visual designers throughout the visual design process to ensure the integrity and fluidity of motion, interaction and flow to ensure visual quality.

Data Tracking and Design Retro

Data tracking

Worked with the product manager prior to launch to identify data that would help with the next design iteration versus data that could help validate the design idea, and brought up the numbers for analysis after the campaign to help with the next design iteration. The returned data was generally in line with our suspicions, but the last pass was heavily churned by users.

Design retro

Combine data for design summaries to prepare for the next design iteration.
