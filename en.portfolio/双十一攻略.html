11.11 Strategy Design

What to buy on 11.11? What to play on 11.11? The only official strategy you need to see on the purchase!

Project Introduction

What should I buy on Double Eleven? What's the fun? What's the cheapest time to buy? When can I get the largest amount of coupons? At what time do the brands I want to buy go on sale? In order to solve these problems of users and promote the best results of each promotion, an official guide is essential to help users understand the process of the event and use the reservation mechanism to remind them to buy.

Company and Time

JD | Nov 2016

Role

UX designer, working with visual designers to modify visual design

Result

For the Double Eleven major venues, major brands, major activities to attract users and promote user purchases.

Requirement Analysis

Requirement background

The purpose of the Double Eleven strategy is to inform users of all the offers in the entire Double Eleven event with a clear timeline, lower the threshold of information access, attract users back and enhance user viscosity.

User requirement analysis

What do users need to see in the Strategy?

From the user's point of view, think about what the user actually wants to see, play and get in the Double Eleven.

Output creative solutions

What kind of strategy format do users prefer?

After determining the user needs and the framework of the strategy, we need to further think about what kind of strategy format users prefer. What kind of visual style can attract users' attention more? What kind of visual style can attract users' attention and make them feel interesting? Based on these considerations, I worked with the operation team and brainstormed more than a dozen options, and finally chose the sightseeing elevator.

Design difficulties

  • How to organize the information hierarchy and structure? So that users can quickly find the information they need?
  • How to increase user viscosity and promote user return?
