Family foody

Family-style dining experience

Project Introduction

With the development of the sharing economy, bicycles can be shared, rooms can be shared, parking spaces can be shared, and food can naturally be shared as well. Food is just like accommodation and transportation, and there is a huge potential market for digging deep into the folk food market. Integrating and reusing the idle resources in society and building a platform between food lovers and food makers can create more value, avoid waste and help optimize resources.

Company and Time

Jiangnan University | Sep 2015

Role

User reseacher and UX designer

Result

Design food sharing platform and build a platform for food lovers and food makers.
