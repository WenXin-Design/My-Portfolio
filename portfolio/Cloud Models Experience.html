Group Buying-11.11 Shopping Festival on Alibaba Cloud

How can a B2B company run a group buying Sales Campaign

Overview

B2B company, especially whose profits mainly depends on big clients, seldomly run any social media marketing. However, Alibaba cloud has been using social media marketing(group buying) as a weapon in 11.11 shopping festival, since 2018. It's a exploratory move both in marketing and design. At the 11.11 shopping festival in 2019, I have been trying to overcome the nature short slab of gruop buying campaign in B2B company with design method, and trying to frame the moderate gamification for B2B company.

Background

Group Buying

Emerging number of group buying sales campaigns are commonplace in Chinese e-commerce platform， such as Pinduoduo，Juhuasuan， and in rating apps such as Dianping(Yelp in china). There are great amout of variations in Group Buying rules. Therefore, before we move on to analize group buying, let's try to define "group buying":

Group buying is a social network marketing tool that involves interactions among users.

According to this definition, I conclude 4 basci models of group buying

Group Buying in B2B

  • Target customer：mostly longtail users, rarely big clients
  • Selling products: products with low price
  • Marketing Strategy: Mostly focusing on bringing new customers rather than exsiting customers. That is because enterprise customers' retention rate would be relatively high, so the group buying campaign targeting exsisting customer would not be necessary. As a result, there usually would be threshold of new customer-only.
  • Features: Successful rate of gathering buying partners would be much lower. Because not everyone would find a buying partner when he /she wants to buy a elastic computing service.

Challenges

Because of the low succesful rate, group initiator would experiencing sense of faluire.

On one hand, we have to guarentee the smooth experience and lowering difficulties due to web interaction

On the other hand, there sould be enough stimuli and it sould be the combination of sensory stimuli and actual benefits.

Toolization

Background

We would spend time on same basic moves on every sales campagin. And the waste of human resources is hugh. Therefore, toolization became a must-do.

Toolization process would not finish in one day and it should be a long term job and a job with every designer's effort. The toolization Framwork is designed by other deisgners. And my job is focusing on how to toolize the marketing tool of group buy.

The primary goal at this phase would be a marketing tool that enables marketing ops to run a small size campaign without designer and front-end developer.

Challenges

For the complicated marketing strategy such as group buy, the biggest challenge would be balancing the flexibility and efficiency. If the rules is fixed, it would be hard to cover every campaign in different scenario. If the rule is too flexible, it would be very hard to learn and use.

The solution would be modulazation based on concluded models. Using same modules to compose different scenario.

And what marketing ops would know is the template based on scenarios rather than modules.
